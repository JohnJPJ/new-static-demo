About

The White Star Line and the Cunard Line were two prominent British shipping companies that played major roles in the history of transatlantic ocean liners during the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Their rivalry helped define the "Golden Age of Ocean Liners," with both striving for dominance in luxury, speed, and innovation.
